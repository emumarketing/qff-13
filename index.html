Our Story

Founded in 1992, this year marks the 22nd Anniversary of the University of Oregon Queer Film Festival. One of the first film festivals of its kind in the NorthWest.

The Queer Film Festival aims to support LGBTQ community through films. We are a multicultural, multiethnic festival that is open to many different forms of expression.

Objectives

  • To give a multimedia voice to the LGBTQ community.
  • To bring those voices to a educational setting for exposure and discussion.
  • To showcase the talent of international and domestic filmmakers.
