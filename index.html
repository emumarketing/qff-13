The University of Oregon Queer Film Festival is seeking narrative and documentary films and videos in both short and feature length formats.

Rough cuts and works-in-progress are eligible for submission if an exhibition version will be available by January 15th, 2015. University of Oregon student, faculty, or staff members do not pay submission fees.

  • December 24th | Regular deadline | $20
  • December 28th | Late deadline | $25

UO filmmakers please contact the festival coordinator at Qff@uoregon.edu to receive a fee waiver code.

Our Story

Founded in 1992, this year marks the 23rd Anniversary of the University of Oregon Queer Film Festival. One of the first film festivals of its kind in the NorthWest.

The Queer Film Festival aims to support LGBTQ community through films. We are a multicultural, multiethnic festival that is open to many different forms of expression.

Objectives

  • To give a multimedia voice to the LGBTQ community.
  • To bring those voices to a educational setting for exposure and discussion.
  • To showcase the talent of international and domestic filmmakers.
